During my tenure at Asterdio Incorporated, I led social media campaigns and content creation for Meezmo, a leading e-commerce platform in Nepal offering electronics, gadgets, home appliances, and tech accessories.

I produced festive campaigns, advertisement reels, product reviews, and voiceover videos. One of the Valentine’s Day video campaigns gained significant popularity, showcasing creative storytelling while promoting Meezmo’s products. I also started office culture videos, highlighting the team environment at Asterdio. Testimonials from new employees revealed that one reason they joined the company was seeing our engaging social media content. A video featuring the character 'Kiran' went viral with over 1,700 shares.

Content Strategy & Execution

My approach involved planning campaigns with a mix of product showcases, festive promotions, and engaging reels. By analyzing audience insights and engagement metrics, I optimized content for maximum reach and brand recall.

  • Produced reels, product reviews, voiceovers, and festive campaigns for Meezmo
  • Collaborated with Nepal’s top tech brands for product promotions
  • Optimized content using engagement metrics to increase reach

This project highlights my ability to combine creative storytelling, data-driven insights, and strategic content planning to deliver measurable results and strengthen brand presence online.
