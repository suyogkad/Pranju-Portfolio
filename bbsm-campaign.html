Driving Record-Breaking Sales on Valentine’s Campaign

Led a nationwide Valentine’s Day campaign across 26 high-volume hypermarkets, achieving a 31.75% overall sales increase and 121.38% growth at the top-performing Hetauda branch.

Bhatbhateni Holdings Limited is Nepal’s largest retail chain, operating a nationwide network of high-volume hypermarkets. In 2024, I worked at Bhatbhateni as a Business Development Associate, contributing to growth-focused initiatives across multiple store locations.

One of my key projects was leading a nationwide Valentine’s Day sales campaign across 26 HHSM (High-Volume Hypermarket Stores). The campaign was led end-to-end by me, covering ideation, budgeting, marketing strategy, execution planning, and post-campaign performance evaluation.

The objective was to drive measurable sales growth through strategic seasonal positioning rather than relying solely on heavy discounting. Campaign decisions were informed by customer purchasing behavior, past seasonal performance, and store-level sales dynamics.

The results were significant. The campaign achieved an overall sales turnover increase of 31.75% across all participating stores. The Hetauda branch recorded the highest growth of 121.38%, making it the top-performing outlet during the campaign period.

Campaign Strategy & Leadership

I led the campaign with a strong focus on structured planning, efficient budget allocation, and cross-functional coordination. This included working closely with store teams, aligning promotional messaging, and ensuring consistent in-store execution.
